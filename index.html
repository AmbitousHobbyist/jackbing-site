Who I Am

Here for a
good time.

I'm Jack. I run Wildflower (cafe + streetwear), make daily content across YouTube and TikTok, and have spent 10+ years in digital marketing, media, design, and video editing. But honestly? It all comes down to three things I love: creating, learning, and entertaining. That's why I do social media.

Whether it's a gaming playthrough, a new piece of tech, a full tech build, or my Nissan 300ZX project car — I just want to bring you along for the ride of me learning new skills and following my hobbies. Hey, you might even learn a thing or two along the way.

I want to encourage people to always follow their dreams and not be afraid to dive in and learn something new. That's the whole point of the channel — give it a go, have fun, figure it out.

The numbers back it up too. 13,300+ subscribers, 740+ videos, daily uploads, and 3+ years of brand partnerships with companies like TeuTeuf, Fanatee, and Trainwreck Labs. Brands I work with get authentic integration in front of an audience that actually pays attention — not vanity metrics.
